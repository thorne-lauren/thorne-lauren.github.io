A Look in to Teen Culture - the VSCO Girl

by Lauren Thorne

Maybe I’m a little too old to be spending hours scrolling through the video sharing app TikTok, but I definitely enjoy my time there. In addition to making me laugh I am able to get a glimpse at what is going viral so I can stay “hip and cool” and not feel too much like an old person when I am only 30. One trend that has swept the nation is that of the “VSCO girl”, particularly popular with teenage girls. VSCO is a popular social media app that allows you to edit and share photos and that is where this trend first started.

Now how do you look like a VSCO girl? The three main ingredients are scrunchies, oversized tee-shirts, and Hydroflasks. Scrunchies have been coming back with a vengeance especially since the rise of the VSCO girl. Going by Claire’s at the mall the other day, I saw a huge portion of their wall display dedicated to scrunchies. And one scrunchie is not enough for a VSCO girl. You can have one in your hair and at least one or two on each wrist as well.

The oversized shirt/hoodie is a trend I am thankful for because it has been my go-to for lounging around my house for years. Now I can look trendy while scrolling through TikTok on my couch! This was also sparked by artists like Billie Eilish who mostly wears oversized clothes, wanting people to focus more on her music than showing off her body (we need more forward thinking artists like this!)

Lastly, is the Hydroflask. VSCO girls are concerned about the environment so they want to do their part to “save the turtles” using metal straws and reusable water bottles to help improve the environment by using less waste. It is great seeing young people these days care about the environment and have it become trendy too!
