Site Purpose

The purpose of SweaterWeather.info will be to provide information about the local weather, specifically what kind of outwear would be appropriate for the weather on any given day. It will provide weather information regarding Preston, Soda Springs, Fish Haven, Storm Center, and will feature a gallery. Visitors can get information about the current weather and the weather for the future so they can make the right plans and wear clothing that will keep them comfortable. They will also find information about the towns with various articles and photos.
