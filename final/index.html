Temple Spotlight: Seattle Washington

The Seattle Washington temple in Bellevue, Washington was the 19th dedicated temple in operation and the very first in the Pacific Northwest. It was dedicated in November of 1980 by Spencer W. Kimball. It is one of the few temples with a west facing statue Moroni who is also holding the gold plates.

The Seattle Washington temple has clothing rental and a cafeteria available for those serving in the temple.
